    Policies

    • Reservations:
      We require advance booking to ensure vehicle availability, especially during peak seasons.Reservation requests are subject to confirmation based on vehicle availability.
    • Cancellation Policy:
      Customers can cancel their reservation without any charge up to 2 days before the scheduled rental start time.
    • No Show Policy:
      If the customer fails to show up for the reservation without prior notice, the reservation will be considered a "no-show," and the entire booking amount may be charged.
    • Booking Confirmation:
      Once the reservation is confirmed, customers will receive a booking confirmation email containing important rental details.
    • Refunds:
      Refunds for eligible cancellations will be processed within 3 days from the cancellation request date. Refunds will be made to the original payment method used during the reservation.
